Salmon with Brown Sugar Glaze

This baked salmon shimmers with a sweet brown sugar and Dijon glaze—some spots on top crisp up thanks to the high oven temp. It bakes in 12 minutes tops and is full of health benefits!

Serve this versatile baked salmon for an elegant dinner party or and as I often do, for a quick and healthy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp of light brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp of dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp of honey
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 salmon filets

How to Prepare:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 425°F
  2. Prepare the glaze
    • In a small bowl, mix the brown sugar, mustard, honey, pepper and salt together until smooth
  3. Dry and glaze the salmon
    • Make sure you remove all of it's skin before doing this!
  4. Bake and serve!
    • Bake until golden brown.The salmon should feel firm when pressed with your fingers. It should take around 12-14 minutes, or 10 minutes if the fillet is less than 1 inch thick. Once done, simply serve and enjoy!
